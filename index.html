No-Code Algo Trading lets you design and deploy trading bots without writing code. Imagine building strategies like assembling LEGO — drag, drop, and connect blocks visually.

  • ⚡ Build strategies faster
  • 📊 Focus on trading ideas, not syntax
  • 🛠 Test instantly before going live
✨ The future of trading is visual.

Build → Test → Deploy with zero code.
